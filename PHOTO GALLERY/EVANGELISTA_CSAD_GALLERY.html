Pictures serve as reminders of our entire experience. Every time we look at it, it tells a different story and evokes a different feeling. Photographs have the capacity to take us back in time and awaken strong memories that we may have forgotten, whether it's of a loved one or a stunning environment. They serve as a reminder of how far we've come and are a visible symbol of our experiences. By taking pictures, I can at least have a recollection of someone I meet along the journey or a memory of an occasion for when I want to look back on it.
